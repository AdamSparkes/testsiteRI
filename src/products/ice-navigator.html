Ice Navigator

Advanced radar imaging and targeting designed for safer and more efficient ice operations in changing maritime conditions.

Why Ice Navigator

Enhanced Ice Awareness

Uses advanced algorithms to improve radar visualization of ice floes, hazards, and changing ice conditions at longer ranges.

Operational Safety and Efficiency

Built for ice management, icebreaking, adventure tourism, research, and port access with safety and environmental responsibility in mind.

Seamless Integration

Passively integrates with existing radar systems, supports dual-radar switching, and uses coastline mapping to reduce false positives near land.

System Overview

Reliable detection and tracking for ice operations.

Ice Navigator adds another layer of protection to overall ice management by enhancing detection at longer ranges and supporting up to 32 alarmed guard zones.

Dedicated targeting supports up to 300 simultaneous tracks, including icebergs, bergy bits, and growlers, helping operators avoid dangerous and costly collisions.

Optional Sea Ice Drift Analysis provides visibility into ice-floe movement within radar range for improved situational understanding and planning.

Key Features

Enhanced Imaging and Targeting

  • Reliable iceberg detection in pack ice
  • Up to 300 simultaneous tracks
  • Detection focus on icebergs, bergy bits, and growlers

Sea Ice Drift Analysis (Optional)

  • Visualizes movement of ice floes within radar range
  • Helps operators anticipate drift and route impact
  • Available for X-band radars

SeaFusion

  • Combines data from up to four onboard radars
  • Supports obstruction avoidance and full 360-degree awareness

Remote Viewing and Control

  • Remote operation under low-bandwidth conditions
  • Capture on-screen or raw radar data
  • Playback recorded raw data as if live for training and analysis
